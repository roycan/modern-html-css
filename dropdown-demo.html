Ryohei Arisu

Arisu is the main protagonist of *Alice in Borderland*. A disaffected young man who finds himself trapped in a deadly alternate Tokyo where he must play survival games to live. His intelligence and problem‑solving skills help him navigate the challenges.

Yuzuha Usagi

Usagi is a skilled mountain climber who partners with Arisu. Her resilience and optimism balance Arisu’s doubts, and together they form a strong bond while facing the brutal games of Borderland.

Shuntarō Chishiya

Chishiya is a cunning and enigmatic player. He often manipulates situations to his advantage, preferring strategy over brute force. His calm demeanor hides a sharp mind and a willingness to outwit opponents.

Kuina Hikari

Hikari Kuina (水鶏 光, Kuina Hikari) is a major character in Alice in Borderland. She was a former Clubs specialist but is currently a Spades.
