Game Instructions

Welcome to Apple Clicker! Here’s how to play:

  • Click Start to begin the game.
  • Click the apple image to earn points. Each click adds 1 point to your score.
  • You can click as many times as you want to increase your score.
  • Click Reset to set your score back to zero.
  • Hover over buttons or the apple to see quick tooltips.

Try to get the highest score possible — and have fun!
